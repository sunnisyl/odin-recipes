Sunni's Lemon Dressing

I could eat this on pretty much anything but you GOTTA try it with my world famous Spaghetti Salad!

Ingredients

  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. All all ingredients to a blender safe caraffe and blitz with an immersion blender.
  2. Drizzle over salad and enjoy!
