Chocolate Chip Shortbread Cookies

This shortbread cookie recipe is quick and easy to whip up with just a few ingredients you probably already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 tspn vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter in the microwave (about 45 seconds) and allow to cool.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and powdered sugar.
  4. Add butter and vanilla and mix until well combined. Mixture will be crumbly.
  5. Add chocolate chips. I like to mix the dough with my hands at this point.
  6. Form dough into 1 inch balls or use a cookie dough scoop and drop onto prepared baking sheets, about 12 cookies per sheet.
  7. Bake cookies for 12 minutes then allow to cool on a wire rack.
