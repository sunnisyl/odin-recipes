Sunni's Spaghetti Salad

Got leftover spaghetti but looking for a salad? Meet spaghetti salad!

Ingredients

  • 1 heaping scoop of leftover spaghetti with sauce
  • 4-5 small meatballs, diced
  • 2 cups greens of choice. I prefer arugula
  • 2 tablespoons of

Instructions

  1. Top salad greens with spaghetti and meatballs.
  2. Drizzle Lemon Dressing over salad and enjoy!
